# Create a campaign

This guide walks you through creating your first FluenzR campaign.

## Step 1: Access creation

1. In the sidebar, click on **Campaigns**
2. Click on the **+ New campaign** button

## Step 2: Configure basic settings

### General information

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Name** | Internal campaign identifier | "Q1 2025 Prospecting - Fintech" |
| **Description** | Explanatory note (optional) | "3 email sequence for LinkedIn leads" |

### Email account

Select the email account that will send the messages:
- If you have multiple accounts, automatic rotation is enabled (Starter+)
- Verify the account is properly connected and active

### Sending settings

| Setting | Description | Recommendation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Sending days** | Authorized days of the week | Mon-Fri |
| **Sending hours** | Time range | 9am-6pm |
| **Timezone** | Time reference | Europe/Paris |
| **Max emails/hour** | Hourly limit | 20-30 |

> **Info**
Timing settings allow you to send your emails at the most relevant times and avoid spam filters.

## Step 3: Create the workflow

The workflow editor opens automatically.

### Add the first email

1. The **Start** node is already present
2. Click on **+ Email** in the toolbar
3. Connect the Email node to Start

### Configure the email

Click on the Email node to edit it:

#### Subject

```
{{firstName}}, a quick question about {{company}}
```

#### Content

```
Hello {{firstName}},

I'm reaching out because I noticed that {{company}}
is expanding its activity in the {{sector}} sector.

At FluenzR, we help companies like yours automate
their prospecting while maintaining a personalized approach.

Would you be available for a 15-minute call this week?

Best regards,
[Your signature]
```

#### Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Open tracking** | Enable open tracking |
| **Click tracking** | Enable click tracking |

### Add a delay

1. Click on **+ Delay**
2. Configure the duration (e.g., 3 days)
3. Connect after the first email

### Add follow-up emails

Repeat the process to create a sequence:

```
Email 1 → Delay 3 days → Email 2 → Delay 5 days → Email 3
```

### Finalize the workflow

Add an **End** node to mark the end of the sequence.

## Step 4: Add contacts

### Method 1: From a group

1. Click on **Add contacts**
2. Select the **Groups** tab
3. Check the desired group(s)
4. Click **Add**

### Method 2: Individual selection

1. Click on **Add contacts**
2. Select the **Contacts** tab
3. Use filters if needed
4. Check the desired contacts
5. Click **Add**

### Verification

Before continuing, verify:
- The number of contacts added
- No duplicates with other active campaigns
- All have a valid email address

## Step 5: Preview

Before launching, preview your emails:

1. Click on **Preview**
2. Select an example contact
3. Verify that variables are properly replaced
4. Check the email rendering

> **Warning**
Send a test email to your own address to check the rendering in your inbox.

## Step 6: Launch the campaign

1. Click on **Start campaign**
2. Review the summary:
   - Number of contacts
   - Number of emails in the sequence
   - Sending settings
3. Click **Confirm**

## After launch

### Real-time tracking

- First emails go out according to your settings
- Check the **Statistics** tab to track metrics
- Replies appear in the **Inbox**

### Possible actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pause** | Temporarily suspends sending |
| **Resume** | Restarts a paused campaign |
| **Add contacts** | Integrates new contacts |
| **Archive** | Archives the completed campaign |

## Tips for an effective campaign

### Email subjects

- Personalize with the first name or company
- Avoid spam words (FREE, URGENT, etc.)
- Keep it short (< 50 characters)
- Test with A/B testing

### Content

- Get straight to the point
- One question/action per email
- Personalize as much as possible
- Avoid large attachments

### Timing

- Follow up at reasonable intervals (3-5 days)
- Limit to 3-4 emails maximum per sequence
- Send during business hours

### Signature

- Include your contact information
- Add your LinkedIn photo if relevant
- Keep a clean and professional signature
