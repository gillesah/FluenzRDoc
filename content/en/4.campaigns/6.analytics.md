# Analytics

FluenzR offers comprehensive analytics to measure and optimize your campaigns.

## Overview

### Campaign dashboard

For each campaign, access statistics by clicking on the **Statistics** tab.

### Main metrics

| Metric | Description | Formula |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Sent** | Number of emails sent | - |
| **Delivered** | Emails that reached destination | Sent - Bounces |
| **Opens** | Contacts who opened | Unique opens |
| **Clicks** | Contacts who clicked | Unique clicks |
| **Replies** | Contacts who replied | Detected replies |
| **Unsubscribes** | Unsubscribed contacts | - |
| **Bounces** | Undelivered emails | - |

## Interpret your results

### Good open rate, low click rate

**Diagnosis:** The subject attracts but the content doesn't convince.

**Actions:**
- Improve your value proposition
- Add a clearer CTA
- Test shorter content

### Low open rate

**Diagnosis:** The subject doesn't encourage opening.

**Actions:**
- Personalize the subject with variables
- Test different approaches (question, number, etc.)
- Check your sender reputation

### Many opens, few replies

**Diagnosis:** Prospects are interested but not convinced.

**Actions:**
- Simplify your ask (short call)
- Add social proof
- Make the action easier (Calendly link)

### High bounce rate

**Diagnosis:** List quality is problematic.

**Actions:**
- Clean your contact list
- Use an email validation service
- Check your collection sources

## Reporting

### Automatic report

Receive a weekly report by email:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Enable **Campaign report**
3. Choose the frequency

### Custom report

Create custom reports:
1. In the campaign, click on **Report**
2. Select the period
3. Choose the metrics
4. Export to PDF or CSV

## Campaign comparison

Compare performance across multiple campaigns:

1. Go to **Campaigns**
2. Select the campaigns to compare
3. Click **Compare**

| Metric | Campaign A | Campaign B | Gap |
|--------|------------|------------|-----|
| Opens | 32% | 28% | +14% |
| Replies | 3.2% | 2.1% | +52% |
