# Introduction to FluenzR

FluenzR is a modern cold emailing platform designed for entrepreneurs, freelancers, and sales teams who want to automate their outreach while maintaining a personalized approach.

## What is FluenzR?

FluenzR allows you to:

- **Connect your email accounts** (Gmail, Outlook, SMTP) securely
- **Manage your contacts** with groups, tags, and custom fields
- **Create automated email sequences** with our visual editor
- **Track your performance** with detailed statistics (opens, clicks, replies)
- **Improve your deliverability** with our automatic warming system

## Why choose FluenzR?

### Simplicity
Intuitive interface, no need to be a technical expert to create effective campaigns.

### Deliverability
Our warming system and built-in best practices maximize your chances of landing in the inbox.

### GDPR Compliance
Automatic unsubscribe management, respect for personal data, and full transparency.

### Personalization
Dynamic variables, custom fields, and A/B testing for emails that convert.

## Architecture

FluenzR uses a modern architecture:

- **Secure OAuth**: Your email credentials are never stored (except for SMTP)
- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted with AES-256
- **REST API**: Integrate FluenzR with your existing tools (Pro Plan)
