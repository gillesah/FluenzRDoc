FluenzR offers three plans tailored to different needs, from beginner freelancers to sales teams.

## Free Plan

Perfect for **discovering** FluenzR and testing the platform.

### Included
- 1 connected email account
- Up to 300 contacts
- 50 emails per day maximum
- Basic workflow editor
- Open and click tracking
- "Sent with FluenzR" signature required

### Limitations
- No warming (higher spam risk)
- No A/B testing
- Basic statistics only

## Starter Plan - $19/month

Ideal for **freelancers** and **independent professionals** who want to professionalize their outreach.

### Included
- 3 email accounts (rotation possible)
- Unlimited contacts
- Unlimited emails (according to your provider)
- Automatic email warming
- A/B testing
- Advanced analytics
- Customizable signature
- Email support

### Use cases
- Freelancers in business development
- Consultants who prospect regularly
- Small businesses with 1-2 salespeople

## Pro Plan - $49/month

Designed for **sales teams** and **agencies** that need advanced features.

### Included
- Everything in Starter +
- 12 email accounts (automatic rotation)
- Up to 5 team members
- Full REST API access
- Outbound webhooks
- Conditional workflows (If opened → email B)
- Custom tracking domain
- Priority support (< 24h)

### Use cases
- Structured sales teams
- Prospecting agencies
- Growing startups

## How to change plans?

1. Go to your **Profile** (icon at bottom left)
2. Click **Manage subscription**
3. Choose the new plan
4. Complete payment via Stripe

> **ℹ️ Info**
Plan changes take effect immediately. If you upgrade, you'll be charged pro-rata. If you downgrade, the new rate applies at the next billing period.

## Payment and Billing

- Secure payment via **Stripe**
- Monthly billing
- Cancel anytime
- Invoices available in the Stripe portal
