# Quick Start

This guide walks you through setting up FluenzR and launching your first email campaign.

## Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [app.fluenzr.co](https://app.fluenzr.co)
2. Click **Sign up**
3. Fill in the form with your email and password
4. Validate your email (check your spam folder if needed)

## Step 2: Connect your email account

FluenzR needs an email account to send your campaigns.

### Option A: Gmail (recommended)

1. In the menu, click **Email Accounts**
2. Click **+ Gmail**
3. Sign in to your Google account
4. Authorize FluenzR to access your account

> **ℹ️ Info**
You may see an "Unverified app" warning. This is normal, our Google certification is in progress. Click "Advanced settings" then "Go to FluenzR".

### Option B: Outlook

1. Click **+ Microsoft**
2. Sign in to your Microsoft account
3. Authorize the requested permissions

### Option C: SMTP

For other providers, see our [SMTP Configuration](/docs/email-accounts/smtp) guide.

## Step 3: Add your contacts

### Manual creation

1. Go to **Contacts**
2. Click **+ Add contact**
3. Fill in at least the email
4. Click **Save**

### CSV Import

To import multiple contacts:

1. Click **Import CSV**
2. Select your file (format: email, firstName, lastName, company, ...)
3. Review the preview
4. Confirm the import

> **⚠️ Warning**
Make sure you have consent from your contacts before sending them commercial emails.

## Step 4: Create a campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns**
2. Click **+ New campaign**
3. Name your campaign
4. Configure the workflow:

### Add an email

1. Click the **Email** node in the toolbar
2. Write your subject and content
3. Use variables to personalize: `{{firstName}}`, `{{company}}`

### Add a delay

To create a multi-email sequence:

1. Add a **Delay** node (e.g., 3 days)
2. Add a second email
3. Repeat as needed

## Step 5: Add contacts to the campaign

1. Click **Add contacts**
2. Select a group or individual contacts
3. Confirm

## Step 6: Launch the campaign

1. Review your email preview
2. Click **Start**
3. Confirm the launch

> **✅ Success**
Congratulations! Your campaign is running. Track real-time statistics in the **Statistics** tab.
